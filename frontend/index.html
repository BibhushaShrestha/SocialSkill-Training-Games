Understanding the Journey

For many autistic children, the social world is like a puzzle with missing pieces. Subtle shifts in tone or facial expressions can feel like a foreign language.

Social Steps provides a safe, digital playground where children can rehearse these moments without the pressure of real-time interactions. Through nurturing quizzes and clear visual anchors, we help bridge the gap between confusion and connection.
